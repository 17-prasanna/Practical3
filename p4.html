Demonstrating Inline, Internal, and External CSS

Inline CSS.
An economy is an area of the production, distribution and trade, as well as consumption of goods and services. In general, it is defined as a social domain that emphasize the practices, discourses, and material expressions associated with the production, use, and management of resources.

Internal CSS.
In economics, expansion is a period of economic growth when the economy is thriving and growing. It's a phase in the business cycle that's marked by an increase in production and employment.

External CSS.
A recession is a period of economic decline that's marked by a decrease in output and an increase in unemployment.
